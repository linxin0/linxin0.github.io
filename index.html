Research Intersts

My research interests mainly lie in computer vision, especially 3D Vision (Reconstruction), Panoramic Vision (3D and Foundational 2D tasks), and Low-Level Image Processing (Unified Restoration, deraining, and denoising). My short-term goal is to achieve immersive perception and world modeling through panoramic information, enhancing intelligent agents’ understanding and interaction with complex environments.
